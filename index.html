Whole Brain

Short Answer: I think

My brain works in a circular motion, passing things back and forth from one side of the brain to the other. Generally, my left brain dislikes something logical, which sets off an emotion or something that is based in feeling on the right side and then returns it to the left. It becomes a war between hemispheres that can make for some fun arguments.
